Effective Leadership Techniques

1. We vs Me mindset.

2. Make others shine by providing them
with tools they need.

3. Understand your team's streangths
and weaknesses.

4. Encourage the team to participate
in the creation of a vision.

5. Center your leadership around the
teammembers' needs.

6. Look for growth not simple completion
of goals.

7. Make them excited about the project.
